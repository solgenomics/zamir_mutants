The 'Bivariate function' plots the values of two selected traits as X and Y values. 90% confidence ellipse is drawn. Points on the external space of the ellipse are considered as unique phenotypes and the names of the corresponding lines are written. Values on both X and Y axes are presented as from the common control, M82. The correlation (R) value and its significance are presented in the Table. The number of means (N) used for the analysis is shown as well.
