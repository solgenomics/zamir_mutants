All phenotypes that were measured over the different experiments

 

Yield related traits :

 

PW – Plant Weight : fresh weight of plant canopy. Presented as Kg per M2.

TY – Total Yield : total fruit yield  measured for each plant or plot. Presented as Kg per M2.

BM – Biomass : Total biomass. Calculated as TY+PW. Presented as Kg per M2.

BX – Brix (TSS) : Total Soluble solids. Measured on sample from 10 fruits per plant/plot. Presented as precentage.

BX*Y – Brix*Total yield : Presented per M2. To obtain the value in g/ M2 multiply by ten.

HI – Harvest Index : calculated  as [ TY/(TY+PW) ]*100. Presented as precentage.

%RED – percent red yield : calculated as the percent of red fruit yield from total fruit yield (red & green).

 

Fruit morphology :

 

FW – Fruit Weight : average fruit weight as measured from a random sample of 20 fruits per plant/plot. Presented as g per fruit.

LEN – average fruit length. Measured  on three fruits per plant. Presented in mm, except for data from 2000-faculty, where  measurements were taken on fruit scans and units are arbitrary.

WID – average fruit width. Measured  on three fruits per plant. Presented in mm, except for data from 2000-faculty, where  measurements were taken on fruit scans and units are arbitrary.

LEN/WID – fruit length divided by fruit width

PER – pericarp thickness. Measured  on three fruits per plant. Measurement was taken at the widest spot on one locule per fruit. Presented in mm, except for data from 2000-faculty, where  measurements were taken on fruit scans and units are arbitrary.

SW – seed weight. Average  seed weight  from a 50 seeds sample, that were extracted from five fruits of a single plant. Presented as weight (g) of 1000 seeds.

IC - Internal fruit color : measured as subjective ratings  for the intensity of the color. Range is from 1 to 5 where  1 is light color  and 5 is dark.

EC – External fruit color : measured as subjective ratings  for the intensity of the color. Range is from 1 to 5 where  1 is light color  and 5 is dark.

L* – lightness

a* - chromaticity coordinates -green-to-red scale,

fruit biochemistry

 

CAR – carotene  content in the fruit. Units are mg/g fruit

LYC – lycopene content in the fruit. Units are mg/g fruit
